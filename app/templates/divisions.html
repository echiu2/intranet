The IT division is responsible for innovation, automation, achieving operational efficiencies, standardization, using industry best practices and maximizing the use of technology across the agency.

The Legal unit/Agency Attorney reviews legal matters, conducts interviews, prepares cases and represents the agency at administrative hearings and in court as needed.

The Municipal Archives preserves and makes available the historical records of New York City government. The MA holds over 200,000 cubic feet and over 50TB of records dating from the early seventeenth century to the present.

The Municipal Library is mandated by the New York City Charter as the official repository for New York City agency publications. They provide access through our print collection and electronic government documents portal.
