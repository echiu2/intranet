DORIS Intranet Use Policy Close

DORIS community standards for its employee intranet create a balance between encouraging communication and ensuring a welcoming and safe platform.

Key principles:

  • Respectful behavior. Sexually explicit, hateful, and violent content is prohibited. Posts with such content will be removed.

  • Support for cultural diversity. Demeaning or threatening posts on the basis of race, ethnicity, national origin, sexual orientation, gender, gender identity, religious affiliation, age, disability, or disease will be removed.

  • Zero tolerance. Posts that advocate behavior that endangers people, whether real-world violence or bullying will be removed.

  • Follow the law. Posts that further unlawful purposes or illegal activities will be removed.

  • Not for commercial purposes or gambling. Post DORIS-related content only. The Intranet should not be used for any commercial purpose. Employees may not post ads for products, services, charities or outside meetings. No chain letters. No gambling, lottery pools, March Madness or other basketball/football or other sports pools.

  • Keeping you safe. No anonymous posting. By using the Intranet, employees consent to review of their use of the intranet and acknowledges there is no expectation of privacy with respect to employee use of the intranet.

  • Take action. Report inaccurate or inappropriate posts to human resources or an EEO officer.

  • Employees who violate this Intranet policy will lose the ability to post on the intranet and may face disciplinary action.
